Business model:
FlowShift Staff is a companion app for event staffing teams. Managers invite
staff to their team, assign shifts, and track hours. Staff use the app to
accept shifts, clock in/out, chat with managers, and track earnings.

Subscriptions are available in two ways:
- Individuals can subscribe via in-app purchase (Starter or Pro tiers)
- Businesses can purchase Starter access for their staff through our website
  (flowshift.work/business), which is applied server-side

Both paths grant the same Starter-tier features. Pro tier with additional
features (more AI messages, caricatures) is available exclusively via IAP.
